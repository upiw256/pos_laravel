# DOKUMEN PERENCANAAN SISTEM POINT OF SALE (POS) KOMPLEKS
# LEVEL: ADVANCE (ENTERPRISE / MULTI-OUTLET)

Sistem POS ini dirancang untuk mencakup seluruh alur bisnis retail atau grosir secara komprehensif, mulai dari Manajemen Master Data, Pembelian, Stok Barang (Inventory), Penjualan (Kasir), hingga Akuntansi (Laporan Laba Rugi).

==================================================
1. FITUR & MODUL UTAMA
==================================================

A. Manajemen Pengguna & Hak Akses (RBAC)
   - Role: Super Admin, Manajer Toko, Gudang (Inventory Manager), Kasir.
   - Manajemen Multi-Outlet/Cabang (opsional jika dibutuhkan).

B. Manajemen Master Data
   - Kategori & Sub-Kategori Produk.
   - Merk / Brand.
   - Satuan Barang (UoM) - Mendukung multi-satuan (Misal: 1 Dus = 12 Pcs).
   - Produk (Single Product, Variant Product seperti Warna/Ukuran, dan Combo).
   - Supplier (Pemasok) & Customer otomatis tersimpan.

C. Manajemen Pembelian (Procurement)
   - Purchase Order (PO) ke Supplier.
   - Penerimaan Barang (Receive Purchase / Good Receipt / GRN).
   - Retur Pembelian (Purchase Return).

D. Manajemen Stok Barang (Inventory Control) - *Sangat Krusial*
   - Kartu Stok (Stock Ledger) riwayat pergerakan in/out barang secara akurat.
   - Penyesuaian Stok (Stock Opname / Adjustment).
   - Transfer Stok Antar Gudang/Cabang.
   - Perhitungan HPP (Harga Pokok Penjualan) dengan metode FIFO atau Average (Penting untuk Laba Rugi).
   - Pengingat Stok Minimum (Low Stock Alert).

E. Manajemen Penjualan (POS Cashier)
   - Antarmuka Kasir (cepat, responsif, full keyboard shortcut).
   - Mendukung Barcode Scanner & Cetak Struk (Thermal Printer).
   - Fitur Hold/Suspend (Menahan pesanan jika pelanggan pergi sebentar).
   - Diskon Fleksibel (Diskon Per Item, Diskon Total, Voucher).
   - Pajak (PPN).
   - Multi Payment (Cash, Debit/Credit, QRIS/E-Wallet, Piutang/Hutang/Split Payment).
   - Retur Penjualan (Sales Return).

F. Manajemen Keuangan & Pengeluaran (Expenses)
   - Pencatatan Biaya Operasional (Listrik, Air, Gaji Karyawan, Transport).
   - Kategori Pengeluaran.

G. Laporan (Reporting & Analytics) - *Mencapai Level Laba Rugi*
   - Laporan Penjualan (Harian, Bulanan, Berdasarkan Produk/Kategori/Kasir).
   - Laporan Pembelian & Hutang Supplier.
   - Laporan Stok Akhir & Nilai Inventaris.
   - Laporan Barang Terlaris (Best Seller).
   - Laporan Pajak.
   - LAPORAN LABA RUGI (PROFIT AND LOSS):
     » Pendapatan Kotor (Total Sales)
     » Harga Pokok Penjualan (HPP / Cost of Goods Sold)
     » Laba Kotor (Gross Profit = Total Sales - HPP)
     » Pengeluaran Operasional (Expenses)
     » Laba Bersih (Net Profit = Laba Kotor - Expenses)

==================================================
2. DESAIN DATABASE (GAMBARAN ERD CORE)
==================================================

- users (id, name, email, password, role_id, outlet_id)
- products (id, name, sku, barcode, category_id, brand_id, unit_id, is_variant, created_at)
- product_variants (id, product_id, name, sku_variant, barcode, additional_price)
- product_prices (id, product_id, variant_id, base_cost, sell_price, active_from)
- inventory_stocks (id, product_id, variant_id, outlet_id, quantity, location)
- stock_movements (id, product_id, variant_id, type(in/out/adj), qty, ref_type, ref_id)
- purchases (id, reference_no, supplier_id, date, status, payment_status, total_amount)
- purchase_items (id, purchase_id, product_id, cost, quantity, subtotal)
- sales (id, reference_no, user_id, customer_id, total_price, tax, discount, grand_total, status, payment_method)
- sale_items (id, sale_id, product_id, variant_id, unit_price, cost_price, quantity, subtotal)  *catatan: cost_price disimpan saat ini untuk hitung laba bersih*
- expenses (id, category_id, amount, date, description, user_id)
- customers (id, name, phone, email, address, total_points, debt_balance)
- suppliers (id, name, contact_person, phone, address)
